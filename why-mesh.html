Interconnect Your Devices

Normally, your phone, laptop, and smart TV cannot talk to each other when they are on different Wi-Fi networks (e.g. phone on mobile data, TV on home broadband). MalikNode creates a secure virtual private LAN. All devices receive a secure static IP within the 10.66.66.0/24 subnet.

Access Smart TVs & NAS Remotely

By disabling Client Isolation, your devices can communicate directly. Stream media files, ssh into servers, or control smart home applications remotely just as if you were sitting on your living room couch.

Encrypted Offline File Sharing

Transfer large files securely between your laptop and server without uploading them to public cloud storage providers. MalikNode routes traffic point-to-point via hardened WireGuard UDP protocols.

No Port-Forwarding Vulnerabilities

Connecting to local resources doesn't require exposing public ports on your home router. MalikNode establishes outbound connections securely to our cloud relays, shielding your endpoints from botnet port scans.
