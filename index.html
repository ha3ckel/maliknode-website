Common Questions

Everything you need to know about MalikNode VPN.

Traditional WireGuard servers require complex command-line configurations, manual generation of server and client keys, MTU calibrations, and custom firewall configuration. MalikNode automates all system processes, serving clean, ready-to-run configurations and QR codes.
For business environments, letting devices ping each other over a VPN poses a security threat if one machine is compromised. We enforce strict firewall filters inside the WireGuard interface that isolate each client connection. They can route outbound packets to the internet securely, but cannot trace or communicate with other client nodes on the VPN.
Since we utilize native WireGuard configurations, you can use the official, free WireGuard application client available on Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, and Linux to import your configurations directly.
